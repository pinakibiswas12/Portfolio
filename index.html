I am Dr. Pinaki Biswas, a Microbiologist and Project Research Scientist with more than 10 years of experience in molecular biology, infectious disease research, and computational biology. My expertise includes PCR/qPCR, cloning, protein interaction studies, docking and molecular dynamics simulation, comparative genomics, and genome sequencing analysis. I have worked extensively on pneumococcal meningitis surveillance under ICMR-NIRBI, published multiple peer-reviewed papers, contributed book chapters, and served as an editorial board member and reviewer for several international journals.

My research goal is to integrate wet-lab experimental microbiology with bioinformatics and molecular simulation approaches to advance translational microbiology and public health–focused innovations.
